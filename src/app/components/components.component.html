Women In His Presence

A Faith-based platform that inspires trans-generational women leaders for the 21st century

Community for You

Women In His Presence fosters a community that inspires women, regardless of their backgrounds, to discover and pursue their God-given purpose. We encourage women to become audacious and intentionally identify their seeds of greatness, develop them into fruits, and serve them to the world.

We promote the posture of daily submission to God and continuous renewing of our minds, replacing false and lying thoughts with the truth and revelation in God's Word.

We Have 3 Core Principles

Connect

Women In His Presence creates various avenues through which women can connect with other women, connect with resources and connect with their SOURCE. We facilitate various meetings, workshops, and conferences during the year.

Empower

Women In His Presence inspires women to discover God’s original intent – purpose. We promote atmospheres that encourage engagement, growth, and empowerment.

Transform

Women In His Presence are agents of change. They seek to enhance and improve their spheres of influence by bolding showing up as the best version of themselves.
